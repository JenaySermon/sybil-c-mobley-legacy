Legacy Partners

Built together. Carried forward.

Dr. Mobley's School of Business and Industry was made possible because she brought corporate America, higher education, and her community to the same table. The Legacy Project continues that tradition — and invites the next generation of partners to join it.

Partners · Coming Soon

We anticipate partnering intentionally, soon — to further the legacy of Dr. Mobley.

We are currently in conversation with universities, corporations, civic organizations, and legacy institutions that share our commitment to advancing Dr. Mobley's vision. If your organization is interested in proposing a unique partnership opportunity, we welcome your inquiry below.

I.

Authentic Alignment

Partnerships that carry Dr. Mobley's name must reflect her principles: rigor, equity, and the refusal to settle for less than excellence.

II.

Meaningful Contribution

We partner with organizations prepared to do the work with us — whether that is scholarship, research, archival stewardship, or programmatic investment.

III.

Mutual Benefit

Great partnerships uplift everyone involved. We seek collaborations that serve our mission, our partners' goals, and the generations of students we were built to serve.
